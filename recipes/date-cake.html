Delicious Cake with Dates, Banana & Almonds

For the cake:

200g ground almonds 3 eggs 1,5 ripe banans 200g dates 50ml almond milk 45g cocoa powder 2tsp baking powder 1 pinch of salt

Gluten- and sugarfree delicacy

Healthy and sugar-free baking is trendy, no question about it. This date chocolate banana cake is currently one of our favorites in the bakery. Chocolate meets fruit, more specifically dates and bananas. The combination of real, unsweetened, cocoa powder and the sweetness of fruit makes the cake a fluffy dessert that is not only healthy but also tastes great. Since the cake is made without flour, it is also gluten-free. Instead of flour, we use ground nuts, which give the cake a great and "fudgy" texture.

Preparation steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 170 degrees top and bottom heat. Line a 18 cm diameter dish with baking paper.
  2. our boiling water over the dates and leave to soak for about 20 minutes so that they can be pur√©ed more easily. Then pour off the soaking water, keeping about 50 ml.
  3. Roast the almonds in a pan. Put the soaked dates in the blender with the soaking water, bananas, eggs, almond milk and all the dry ingredients, i.e. the roasted and ground almonds, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt. Puree all the ingredients to form a smooth dough.
  4. Pour the dough into the prepared springform pan and bake in the preheated oven on the middle shelf for about 25-35 minutes.
  5. The finished cake should be firm but not hard. Allow the cake to cool, then remove from the pan, serve and enjoy.
